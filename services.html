Caro Chiropractic –

Gentle, Trusted Care for Your Spine and Wellbeing

At Caro Chiropractic, we welcome you to our centre in Bukit Mertajam, Penang, where care is centred around you. Rooted in the meaning of “Caro” — to care deeply — we are committed to providing chiropractic treatment that feels gentle, reassuring, and effective.

Our goal is not only to reduce pain, but also to improve your range of motion and support your body in moving and functioning at its best. Whether you are seeking relief or looking to maintain an active, comfortable lifestyle, our care is tailored to your individual needs.

Every journey with us begins with a thorough initial consultation. We take time to understand the history of your concern, followed by a detailed assessment and examination. Before any treatment begins, we will clearly explain our findings and recommendations, so you can feel informed and confident in your care.

Most importantly, we prioritise your comfort. Chiropractic treatment is generally gentle and not painful, and we always adapt our approach to suit what feels safe and right for you.

At Caro Chiropractic, every spine is cared for with intention, respect, and a genuine commitment to your well-being.

Our Services & Treatments

Chiropractic Treatment

Chiropractic treatment is a hands-on care that improves spinal alignment and joint function to support the body’s natural healing.

At Caro Chiropractic, we use techniques like Diversified, Thompson Drop Table, Activator, and SOT blocks based on your condition to improve treatment results.

Prenatal & Postpartum Chiropractic Care

Specialised, gentle care supporting women through every stage of pregnancy and postpartum recovery.

Focuses on pelvic stability, spinal comfort, and reducing pregnancy-related strain for a smoother journey into motherhood.

Pediatric Chiropractic Care

Gentle and tailored care for infants, children, and adolescents during their critical growth stages.

Supports healthy spinal development, posture, and nervous system function in a safe and calming environment.

Spinal Decompression Traction

A non-invasive spinal therapy designed to gently relieve pressure on intervertebral discs and nerves.

Helps support recovery from disc injuries, nerve compression, and persistent lower back or neck pain.

Dry Needling

A targeted myofascial technique used to release deep muscular tension and trigger points.

Promotes improved circulation, reduced pain, and faster recovery from musculoskeletal dysfunction.

Corrective Exercises & Rehabilitation

Personalised exercises designed to help your body move better and heal properly.

Helps rebuild strength, improve posture, and support long-term recovery so you can feel more stable and confident in daily life.
